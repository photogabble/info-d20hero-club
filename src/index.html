The aim of d20hero.club is to be an inclusive Pleroma community for everything tabletop. We welcome anyone who wants to be part of the community be you a boardgamer, RPG player, LARPer, artist or other.

This is meant to be a positive and safe space for people to enjoy each other's ideas, opinions and have fun. To keep d20hero.club that way, the code of conduct and rules will be applied and enforced thoroughly and transparently.

Rules, Etiquette, Bots, block list

  • This is a Bot friendly instance, bot accounts are welcome provided they follow these rules
  • You are welcome and encouraged to have an account for your (tabletop related) company or an account for an actual game provided you follow our self promotion guidelines
  • Please browse our code of conduct and rules for a detailed list of what is and isn't acceptable on this instance

Viable alternatives

We welcome main accounts and alternative accounts. If you only have one account on the fediverse, we highly recommend the creation of a second alternative account as a backup.

  • tabletop.social – a Mastodon community with the same goals as d20 Heroes
  • Ten Forward – a Star Trek themed Mastodon community, for in character account, with good moderation
  • The Wandering Shop – a Mastodon instance initially geared for the science fiction and fantasy community but open to anyone
  • Elekk – a Mastodon instance by gamers, for gamers
  • WUBRG – an inclusive community for fans and players of the trading card game Magic: the Gathering
  • dice.camp – a Mastodon instance for tabletop gamers.
  • Boardgames Social – a Mastodon community for boardgamers, has the same goals as d20 Heroes and tabletop.social
