Bot Policy

Last updated on 20th October 2018, adapted from the sleeping.town bot policy available here.

Bot accounts are welcome, provided they follow these rules:

  • All Markov bots or other generative bots that may output nonsense must always post unlisted, or with a CW.
  • If a bot will post public posts, it must not post any more frequently than once an hour and must be clearly marked as a bot in its display name.
  • Bots must never mention other users unless the mentioned user has initiated it. For such user-initiated posts, any rate limits may be ignored.
  • The bot's operator must be included in its description or a pinned post.
  • Top-level unlisted or followers-only posts must not be made any more frequently than every 15 minutes.
  • Exception: Now-playing or scrobble bots may post as frequently as once a minute.
  • If a bot incorporates data from posts made by another user that are not directed at the bot, the bot must be following that user.
  • Bots must follow the same rules as normal users, with regards to acceptable content, harassment, etc.
  • Generative bots must be monitored by their operator.

Failure to follow these rules may result in the bot being suspended. A bot's operator is responsible for all content posted by it.

Open source bots are encouraged, and should have a link to their source code in their description or a pinned post.
