Federation Status

Last updated on 16th December 2018, based upon settings from beeping.town, pleroma.site & glitterkitten

d20 Heroes generally has an open federation policy. However, at the discretion of the instance admin & moderation team, we may modify that policy with some instances. The federated timeline on d20 Heroes is not curated, but there's certain instances we are just not going to deal with.

d20 Heroes participates in MRF transparency. Users may verify the running configuration by viewing our NodeInfo data.

Overall, we try to avoid blocking whole instances, but we have the capability. Instead, we prefer to take less intrusive actions to solve problems. As such, this is our current policy:

Rejected instances

Rejected instances can't interact with us, and their posts won't appear on the public timeline. This is otherwise called defederation and is reserved as the nuclear option.

The reasons for defedetation of the below instances is largely due to strong concerns about data mining and advertising (spam), the allowing of content deemed illegal in the UK (e.g loli) or extreme cases of abuse or harassment with lack of moderation.

Instances with media removal will not have their media (uploaded images/videos) shown inside our instance, but you can still follow the link to their profile to see it.

#NSFW Rewrite

These instances will have their messages containing media marked #nsfw and sensitive automatically. This is due to a large possibility that the message may contain content that violates our policies. In the interests of ensuring that content which does not violate our policies may still be viewed, we mark the images #nsfw and sensitive instead of removing them altogether.
